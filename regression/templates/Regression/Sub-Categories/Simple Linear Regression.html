About Simple Linear Regression

Regression is a statistical method used in finance, investing, and other disciplines that attempts to determine the strength and character of the relationship between one dependent variable (usually denoted by Y) and a series of other variables (known as independent variables.

Requirements

If the dependent variable (y) consists of numeric values and there is only one independent variable (X). Here, we have to use Simple Linear Regression.

Applications

  • Forecasting
    • Linear regressions can be used in business to evaluate trends and make estimates or forecasts.
  • Analysing
    • Linear regression can also be used to analyze the marketing effectiveness, pricing and promotions on sales of a product.
